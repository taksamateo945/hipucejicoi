Zendaya Reveals The Reason For Her Emerald Green Dress Rihanna Might Like Her Possible Next Move

Zendaya has managed to make quite the impression at the latest Emmy Awards, wearing a stunning custom green dress that immediately drew a lot of attention to her.

Many of her fans noted that she looked like Poison Ivy from the DC Universe, while others drew comparisons to Ariel, the mermaid.

In any case, many of the comments pointed out how stunning the young actress looked in her emerald dress and gorgeous red hair.

The overall outfit was done in a very tasteful manner, with many subtle details and accessories topping off the whole look. Some even compared Zendaya to an actual princess.

She was earlier presented with the Fashion Force Award, and it looks like she is doing a good job living up to the expectations set by this recognition.

Zendaya also opened up about her fashion tastes, explaining that it was something very emotional and personal to her, and has allowed her to show a more careless attitude to the world, without worrying so much about people’s opinions and other similar issues.

Unfortunately, Zendaya did not get nominated for any Emmy Awards, although she is reportedly feeling hopeful for next year.
