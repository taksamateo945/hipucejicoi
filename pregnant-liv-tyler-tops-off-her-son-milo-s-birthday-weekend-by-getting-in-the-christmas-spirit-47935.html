Pregnant Liv Tyler Tops Off Her Son Milos Birthday Weekend by Getting in the Christmas Spirit!

What a weekend for Liv Tyler and her son, Milo!

Yesterday, the 37-year-old pregnant actress got into the holiday spirit by posting a cute photo with a lighted Santa Claus Christmas decoration on her Instagram page.

This latest pic comes just days after the social media savvy mom shared several images to commemorate her Milo’s 10th birthday, including a throwback photo of him on the day he was born in 2004.
