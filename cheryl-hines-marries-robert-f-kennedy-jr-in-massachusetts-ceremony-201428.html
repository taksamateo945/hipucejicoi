Cheryl Hines Marries Robert F. Kennedy Jr. in Massachusetts Ceremony

Add her to the family tree! Cheryl Hines married Robert F. Kennedy Jr. at the Kennedy family compound on Cape Cod in Hyannis Port, Mass., on Saturday, Aug. 2, Us Weekly can confirm. The couple said "I do" under a tent on the six-acre Kennedy Compound on Cape Cod.

The bride and groom were feted by 300 guests, including Larry David, Julia Louis-Dreyfus, Kevin Nealon and Ed Begley Jr. Hines, 48, wore a strapless Romona Keveza cocktail length dress and accessorized with a white belt. She wore her blonde locks up with loose strands pulled down around her face. She carried white roses and blue hydrangeas down the aisle, which matched the wedding's color scheme. RFK Jr. donned a black suit and black striped tie to exchange the couple's vows, which lasted 30 minutes.

Hines and Bobby Jr., the son of Ethel and Robert F. Kennedy and nephew of John F. Kennedy, got engaged this spring after two years of dating. News of their engagement was revealed at Muhammad Ali's Celebrity Fight Night in Phoenix, Ariz., on April 29.

The Curb Your Enthusiasm actress recently gushed to Us why she and the attorney are a perfect match. "We share the same values. Family first," she said at the Riverkeeper Fishermen's Ball in NYC in April. "We like to have a good time and work hard."

Hines noted that "it's fun to have a partner" while supporting each other's respective causes, but the pair also cherish their downtime together.
