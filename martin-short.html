Who is Martin Short:

Martin Short is a famous Movie Actor. He was born on March 26, 1950 and his birthplace is Hamilton, Canada. Martin is also well known as, Saturday Night Live comic who starred in the 1996 film, Jungle to Jungle, and the 1986 film Three Amigos. He played roles in the 1996 film Mars Attacks! and the 1994 film Clifford. He also provided his voice for Madagascar 3 in 2012.

Martin is originated from Canada. He appeared in Father of the Bride 1 & 2 with Steve Martin.
