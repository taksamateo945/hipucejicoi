Chris Pine Net Worth: A Journey Through the Actors Wealth and Achievements

Chris Pine has won several awards and nominations for his acting work. Some of his notable awards include:

  • Boston Society of Film Critics Award for Best Ensemble Cast for Star Trek in 2009.
  • Satellite Award** for Best Ensemble – Motion Picture for Into the Woods in 2014.
