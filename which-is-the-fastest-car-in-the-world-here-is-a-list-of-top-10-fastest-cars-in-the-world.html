Which Is The Fastest Car In The World: Here Is A List Of Top 10 Fastest Cars In The World

4. Rimac Nevera

While electric cars are known for instant acceleration due to their electric motors, battery weight often hinders top speed.

However, this car defies this trend.

This hypercar boasts four electric motors, generating a remarkable 1,914hp and 2,300Nm of torque.

With this power, it accelerates from 0-60mph in just 1.9 seconds and can reach a top speed of 258mph.

Its carbon fiber construction keeps weight in check, and despite its incredible performance, it offers a luxurious interior, making it a well-rounded option for those willing to invest in its £2 million price tag.

5. McLaren Speedtail

The Speedtail is not about setting records, but it hits 250mph, McLaren’s fastest road car.

This top speed even surpasses that of the iconic McLaren F1, which was their last three-seat car.

The car’s ultra-aerodynamic design avoids the inclusion of drag-inducing wings, giving it the appearance of a vehicle plucked from a futuristic cyberpunk movie.

6. Aston Martin Valkyrie
