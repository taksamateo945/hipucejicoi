KUWK: Kim Kardashian Confesses She Used To Be Embarrassingly Obsessed With Becoming Famous

Kim Kardashian got candid about her years before becoming such a well-known celebrity and confessed that at the time, she was ‘obsessed’ with fame. That being said, she also admitted that she’s quite embarrassed about having that sort of attitude now.

Kim was actually interviewed by her own hubby, rapper Kanye West for Vogue Arabia, the cover of which she is gracing.

As she chatted with her husband, Kim was a completely open book, answering any personal question thrown her way.

When it comes to her shortcomings, Kim stated that: ‘I try to be a good person and take my mistakes and then make them into a better experience – to learn and to grow from them and move on.’

She went on to give an example of such a mistake, sweetly saying that it’s definitely not dating Kanye from the very beginning of their friendship.

The Kardashian and her entire family are pretty much considered to have royal status in the United States now, but how has she dealt with fame in the past?

Kim admitted that ‘Money was always the goal, but I was just obsessed with fame, like, embarrassingly obsessed… I agree that fame can be addictive.’

Throughout all the Keeping Up with the Kardashians seasons and her social media activity, Kim has shared most of her life with the world.

That being said, does she regret not keeping more under wraps?

‘Even in my darkest times I do not regret putting myself out there for the world to see.
