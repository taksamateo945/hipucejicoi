Giuliana Rancic Looks Slim in Sheer Pants Pants at Grammys 2015

She wears the pants!

We’re used to seeing Giuliana Rancic is long flowing red carpet gowns, but the fashion expert switched it up for the 2015 Grammys on Sunday, Feb. 8, in Los Angeles.

Opting for a more androgynous yet covered up look to make up for her less than stellar Golden Globe Awards ensemble, Rancic hit the carpet in a head-turning jumpsuit. The E! Fashion Police host, 40, sported a black outfit with mesh panels and ornate beading. With her hair pulled pack into a loose chignon, Rancic added a bold red lip for pizzazz.

“I am wearing pants for the first time on the red carpet,” Rancic said of her ensemble. “It’s definitely not faux sheer — there is no such thing,” she clarified to Ryan Seacrest. “It’s sheer all the way up so I couldn’t wear any sort of undergarments.”
