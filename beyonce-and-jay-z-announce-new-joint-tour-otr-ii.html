Beyonc And Jay-Z Announce New Joint Tour, OTR II

After weeks of rumours and press speculation, Beyoncé And Jay-Z have announced plans for a brand new joint world tour, OTR II.

An advertisement video for the powerful couples second joint stadium tour, was uploaded onto Beyoncé official Instagram page earlier today, Monday, March 12th, alongside two of the other promotional images emblazoned with the letters “OTR II”.

Check out the OTR II promotional video and images below:

Presented by Live Nation Global Touring in association with Beyoncé’s Parkwood Entertainment and Jay-Z’s Roc Nation, the OTR II Tour kicks off in Cardiff, Wales on Wednesday, June 6th.

The international leg of the tour will stop in 15 cities across the UK and Europe, before beginning a 21-date run across North American on July 25th, in Cleveland.

Tickets for US and mainland Europe dates go on sale March 19th, while tickets for the UK shows go on sale on March 23rd.

Beyoncé And Jay-Z last toured together back in 2014, for the first On the Run Tour, in support of Beyoncé’s self-titled 2013 record, and Jay-Z’s 12th studio album ‘Magna Carta Holy Grail’.

Grossing $96 million in the first 19 North American shows and $109.7 million in total, the tour was deemed a huge commercial and critical success, leaving fans of the duo wanting more.
