Bun B Defends Family, Shoots Masked Intruder Who Pointed Gun At Wife

The rapper Bun B was forced to take matters into his own hands when he shot a home intruder in his southwest Houston neighborhood on Tuesday.

According to CNN, Bun’s wife Angela “Queenie” Walls opened her front door around 5:45 p.m. after the doorbell rang and found a masked man standing there with a gun.

Before she opened the door, Walls thought it was a delivery person, but it was really 20-year-old DeMonte Jackson, who forced his way in and demanded luxury items.

Walls then directed him to the garage and offered her Audi. Bun eventually heard the ruckus, armed himself and came back downstairs. From there, a gun fight ensued, Jackson was hit and fled the scene.

He later showed up to a Houston-area hospital for medical attention and was later charged with two counts of aggravated robbery and one count of burglary.

And since the shooting took place, people have been leaving numerous comments on the UGK rapper’s page and relayed how glad they are he and Walls are safe.

“Glad you’re good OG,” one person wrote.

“They f—ed with the wrong one,” wrote another.

And considering another prominent rap figure in Nipsey Hussle was murdered less than a month ago, a lot of people were glad another one wasn’t killed.

“Let n—–s be grateful about not losing another legend tho just knowing when somebody in danger of losing their life it’s always a bunch of people that care on another level when a loved one could’ve lost a life,” someone wrote.

“THANK GOD YOU SAFE OG WE DONT NEED ANOTHER LEGEND LOSS MAN 🙏🏾,” another person commented.

Bun and Walls are said to be unharmed, and the rapper hasn’t been charged with anything.
