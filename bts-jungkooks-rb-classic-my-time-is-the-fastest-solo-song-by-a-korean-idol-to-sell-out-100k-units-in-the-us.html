BTS Jungkooks R&amp;B classic My Time is the fastest solo song by a Korean Idol to sell out 100

‘My Time’ by BTS’ Jungkook is now the fastest solo song by a Korean Idol from a group to sell out over 100,000 units in the U.S, achieving it just 57 days after its release. Singer and songwriter Jungkook participated in writing and composing the lyrics of the song. The sexy R&B classic is the 3rd song in BTS’s new album Map of the soul: 7 & the first solo song from BTS to achieve this milestone. 

‘My Time’ is a track from BTS’s 4th full-length album, Map Of The Soul: 7.

Following this achievement, ‘Euphoria’ another melodious song by Jungkook reaches 130M streams, remaining the most streamed BTS solo song. The song made it to the top 50 of longest-charting idol songs on Melon platform at No.25—the only BTS solo song. 

The songs ‘Euphoria’ & ‘My Time’ by Jungkook are two songs that have received great enthusiasm from people globally.

Furthermore, ‘My Time’ ranked 84th on Billboard’s chart ‘Hot 100’ in its first week, peaking second on ‘Digital Song Sales,’ as the only solo from the band BTS to achieve this feat, showing tremendous global popularity. For the seventh week of Billboard’s ‘World Digital Song Sales’, ‘My Time’ ranks third, charting the highest among all BTS songs.  Jungkook might just be the most popular and influential member as the face of BTS.

My Time is an emotional song in which main vocalist Jungkook sings with the tune of R&B vocals, delivering deep feelings of his inner story from his debut life as an Idol. Famous music critic ‘Kim Young-Dae’ declared that he has confirmed Jungkook’s possible career as a R&B Korean soloist by his outstanding song ‘My Time’.
