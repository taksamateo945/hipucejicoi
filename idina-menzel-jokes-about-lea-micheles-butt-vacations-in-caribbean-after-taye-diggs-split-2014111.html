Idina Menzel Jokes About Lea Michele's Bikini Butt Picture

Bum buddies! Lea Michele’s recent bum snapshot has caught the eye of many — including Idina Menzel! Just days after Michele posted a very revealing pic of herself during a Mexico getaway, the Broadway veteran teased that she would like to mimic Michele, her onscreen daughter on Glee.

“On vacation in Caribbean/ desperately want to take a pic of my ass in bikini like @msleamichele but never seems to be the right light.huh,” Menzel, 42, joked on Jan. 9. “I could do it but i never learned how to use photo shop.”

The Frozen actress received plenty of support from fans, but ultimately didn’t go as far as posting her very own snapshot. “I love my fans but you guys are blowing smoke up my…” she added.
