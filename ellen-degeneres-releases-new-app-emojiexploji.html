Ellen DeGeneres Releases New App, #EmojiExploji

Ellen DeGeneres launched her very own line of custom emojis! She joins other celebrities, such as Kim Kardashian, Justin Bieber, and Stephen Curry who also have their own line!

Ellen’s app is called “Emoji Exploji” and includes various emojis that fits her fun personality!

For example, one of the emojis is of her dancing! If you have ever watched an episode of The Ellen Show, you know that Ellen LOVES dancing, especially with her audience members.

The TV host also went on Twitter to give fans a glimpse of her emojis through a video! The short clip shows Ellen doing different types of dances, such as Silento’s “whip and nae nae.”

If you haven’t downloaded my new app #EmojiExploji yet, here’s what you’re missing.https://t.co/XlzfEI76NI

— Ellen DeGeneres (@EllenDeGeneres) August 25, 2016

Other emojis include Ellen boxer-briefs, Ellen with heart eyes, the pride flag, and many more!

Ellen’s app is different from other emoji apps because hers also includes GIFs! Apart from her dancing, Ellen also does a mic drop in a GIF.

Today is gonna be epic. #EmojiExploji
Apple: https://t.co/ZtdTFnktUJ
Android: https://t.co/cxLdndboqv pic.twitter.com/87adsrtj05

— Ellen DeGeneres (@EllenDeGeneres) August 25, 2016

“Emoji Exploji” is available for both Apple and Android users for $1.99!

Fans can also see Ellen on the 14th season of The Ellen Show when it premieres on September 6.
