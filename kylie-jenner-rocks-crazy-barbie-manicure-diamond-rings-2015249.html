Kylie Jenner Rocks Crazy Barbie Nails, Lots of Diamond Rings

Nailed it! After her beau Tyga shouted-out his “fiancee” via Snapchat, Kylie Jenner took to Instagram on Wednesday, Sept. 23, to share a close-up photo of her fierce Barbie-inspired manicure and a whole slew of diamond rings.

Flaunting her custom bubblegum mani, complete with over-the-top ’80s Barbie nail art, Jenner captioned the shot with a shout-out to her manicurist. “Thank you @britneytokyo [L.A.-based nail artist Britney Tokyo] for coming to the house today to do our nails for Jordyns birthday!” she wrote. “I love my Barbie nails.”

While the ultra-femme long tips were eye-catching in their own right, we may have been a little distracted by the number of diamond rings on Jenner’s fingers, including the ring finger on her left hand. On that digit alone, Kendall Jenner‘s little sister rocked four rings: three thin bands, and one thicker band with gold details.
