Shirley Jones (Shirley Partridge)

Throughout her six-decade Hollywood career, Shirley, 83, has starred in musical films including Oklahoma!, Carousel, and The Music Man. Before appearing on The Partridge Family — on which she co-starred with her real-life stepson David Cassidy — the actress won an Oscar for her role in the 1960 movie Elmer Gantry.
