REPORTJosh Elliott and Liz Cho are Having Their Wedding This Weekend!

“I Do” details!

NBC Sports anchor Josh Elliott will reportedly marry his fiancée, WABC journalist Liz Cho, this weekend at an intimate California wedding attended by close family and friends, according to Page Six.

“The wedding will be near Santa Barbara on Saturday night at a private villa that they have rented for a week. It’s not a big showbiz wedding, but a small, romantic affair with their closest friends and family,” a source close to the couple told the publication.

Josh and his daughter, Sarina, in March 2012.

Guests expected at the nuptials include former Good Morning America weatherman Sam Champion and Liz’s co-anchor, Bill Ritter.

Liz and Josh, both 44, became engaged last Labor Day during a romantic getaway to Europe. “He gave her a simple gold band that was a family heirloom and meant a lot to him,” a source told Page Six at the time.

Liz with her daughter, Louisa, in November 2011.

“It’s been very low-key, and people may not have even realized she was engaged because she’s not wearing a standard diamond engagement ring,” the insider added of the bride-to-be’s simplistic gold band.

The couple first started dating in 2012 after Liz split from her first husband, Evan Gottlieb, with whom she has an eight-year-old daughter, Louisa Simone. Josh was also previously married to Priya Narang, with whom he has a five-year-old daughter named Sarina.
